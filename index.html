The design of the 2024/25 home kit, which is available to buy here now!

It takes inspiration from a shirt worn by the Busby Babes in 1952. This was made from reflective materials to aid visibility on the pitch, in the early years of evening games being played under floodlights.

Crafted for performance and to help provide world-class players with the confidence to play under pressure, the lightweight jerseys feature the latest in adidas technology which has been created in close collaboration with players throughout the development process. The on-field version of the shirt is constructed with HEAT.RDY technology, using advanced materials to maximise air flow to keep players feeling cool, while the fan version features AEROREADY technology, which uses sweat-wicking or absorbent materials to keep the body feeling dry.
